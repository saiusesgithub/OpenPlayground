How This Word Clock Works

This clock tells time using words instead of numbers. The illuminated words form sentences like "IT IS HALF PAST TEN IN THE MORNING" or "IT IS A QUARTER TO THREE". The poetic display translates the time into evocative phrases that change with each passing minute, creating a living poem that reflects the nature of time itself.
